Game Information

The game is a simplified version of whacka-mole that increases in difficulty as you gain more points.

Difficulty increase begins after gaining your first 5 points.

Two settings exist, a speed multiplier, which changes how quickly the active button is changed by a multiple of the input,

and points per hit, which is how many points are gained every time an active button is clicked.
